As you work through the module, you might find certain areas where you'd like a little extra support. To help with that, we've created a selection of additional prompts and mini tasks to guide you, along with a set of ChatGPT prompts if you'd like to use AI for extra help. When used intentionally, it's a powerful tool – so don't be afraid to lean on it as an extra team member.

You don't need to complete these in order (or even at all) - just choose the ones that resonate with you. Write, doodle, list, or record voice notes if that feels easier. The aim is to explore, not to perfect.

BRAND STORY & IDENTITY MAP
Draw or design a simple visual map of your journey so far — from when you first became interested in nails to where you are today. Add images, doodles, or keywords that represent key milestones (e.g. your first training day, your first client, your first full book, your first class taught). Then, sketch a “future chapter” — what do you want the next milestone on your story timeline to be?

Imagine you’re chatting with a friend or being interviewed for a podcast. Answer these questions conversationally:
• What inspired you to start doing nails or teaching?
• What makes your approach different from others?
• What do you want your clients or students to feel after working with you?
Speak freely — no script. Then, listen back and highlight the parts that sound most like you. Those are your story anchors.

Gather physical reminders of your journey — photos of your early sets, old training certificates, messages from clients, past branding, or nail designs that marked a turning point. Lay them out in front of you and reflect:
• What patterns do you see?
• How has your style, confidence, or purpose evolved?
• Which parts of your story do you feel most proud of sharing?
If you’re an educator, you could even create a mini “before and after” board showing how you’ve grown — personally and professionally.
